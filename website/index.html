Frequently Asked Questions

How to start a gym franchise with your brand?

Submit the inquiry, complete discovery, review the FDD, and follow our step‑by‑step journey from site selection to grand opening. We provide the playbooks, tech stack, and coaching to accelerate learning and reduce avoidable mistakes when starting a gym franchise.

What is the typical time to open?

From franchise award to opening: generally 6–9 months depending on site availability, permitting, and build‑out timelines.

What are the recurring fees?

Royalty, brand fund, technology platform fee, and required local marketing spend. Exact amounts are disclosed in the FDD.

Who is the ideal candidate?

Growth‑minded operators with people leadership experience, strong financial acumen, and commitment to process. Semi‑absentee is possible with a qualified general manager.

Do you offer protected territories?

Yes. Exclusive territories are granted using population, drive‑time, or trade‑area modeling. Details are defined in the franchise agreement.

Do you help with financing?

We can introduce third‑party lenders and equipment financing partners; final terms are between you and the lender.

Can I own multiple locations?

Yes. We support multi‑unit development with territory planning, shared staffing strategies, and phased openings.

How is this different from other gym franchise options?

Compared to general‑access brands, we emphasize coached experiences, technology‑enabled marketing, and rigorous field support to drive retention and unit economics.
